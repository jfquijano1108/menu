History

The Russian Spring Punch was created in London, England, by Dick Bradsell in the 1980s. He claims to not remember which bar he worked at then, but he tells the story of how he created the recipe for some personal friends who wanted hold a cocktail party while minimizing the amount of money they had to spend on alcohol. The participants received vodka, cassis, sugar syrup and lemon juice, and were asked to bring their own sparkling wine.
